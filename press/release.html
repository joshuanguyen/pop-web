Pop.

Rediscover how awesome your friends are by talking to them with Pop.
Video texting app spotlights personal, expressive conversations.

SAN FRANCISCO - November 13, 2014 - Imagist Labs announced today the immediate availability of Pop for iPhone and iPod Touch users.

Pop is a video texting app for close friends. Conversations on Pop have the expressiveness of video and the convenience of texting.

There's no need to set up a video call to chat with friends. Videos on Pop behave like texts. Users chat in real-time or respond to videos when they want.

Features:

  • Simple to navigate - there’s only one screen for all functions.
  • Press and hold to record and send a video message.
  • Videos last up to 5 seconds.
  • Messages automatically join together to form conversations. Swipe left to see old messages.
  • Messages can be played and replayed indefinitely.
  • Bold pop filters enhance faces. Drag the toggle to change colors. Tap the toggle to switch to black & white.

Research over the past four decades have shown that 93% of human communication comprise of verbal and visual content (the 7%-38%-55% rule1). Pop lets users reclaim the context and subtlety of face to face communication from text messaging.

“I was frustrated with video chat, voicemail, and texting. ” said Alaric Cole, co-founder of Pop. “So I built something to take the best from each of each, with none of the annoyances.”

Pop’s compressed videos are designed with poor network conditions and budget data plans in mind.

Pop is ideal for close friends and family who want a personal and expressive way to talk to one another.

Pricing and Availability
Pop is available in English, Chinese and Spanish and is free on Apple App Store. Pop is designed for iPhones and iPod Touch, running on iOS8 or above.

About Imagist Labs
Imagist Labs designs and builds mobile apps for users to communicate and express themselves visually. The company was founded by Joshua Nguyen, a product leader at Tumblr and Flickr, and Alaric Cole, a serial mobile engineer with several top 15 apps in the App Store.
